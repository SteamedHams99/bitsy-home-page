About Cindy Meister

Cindy Meister is a former attorney who transitioned to being a stay-at-home mom. She has spent her life in the Chicagoland area, where she raised her two beautiful daughters and is now a proud grandmother.

With a passion for storytelling and a keen interest in the evolving role of technology in human relationships, Cindy brings a fresh and insightful perspective to her writing. The Idea of You is her debut book, exploring the intersection of AI and modern dating.
